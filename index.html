About Cotria Cottage - Your Mountain Retreat in Gomismta

Welcome to Cotria Cottage, your peaceful mountain escape in Gomismta, Guria, Georgia. With stunning panoramic views, cozy interiors, and all modern essentials, this cottage is perfect for a relaxing holiday in the Georgian mountains.

Our 4-bedroom mountain cottage includes 1 sofa-bed (accommodates up to 9 guests), a well-equipped kitchen, and a spacious living room with panoramic windows overlooking the breathtaking landscape of Guria region.

Located in the beautiful mountainous region of Guria, Cotria Cottage offers a tranquil getaway for couples, families, and groups. Whether you're here to hike mountain trails, unwind in nature, or enjoy fresh mountain air and cloud-sea views, you'll feel right at home in our mountain retreat.

Location & Directions to Gomismta

Cotria Cottage is located in upper Gomismta, Guria region, Georgia - easily accessible by car year-round. The cottage sits at an elevated position offering spectacular views of the surrounding mountains and occasional glimpses of the Black Sea.

Best time to visit: May to September for dreamlike cloud-sea views and perfect weather for hiking. Experience the natural beauty of Guria region with its hiking trails, pristine mountain air, and rich cultural heritage.

Distance from major cities: Accessible from Ozurgeti (closest city), Batumi, and Kutaisi. Perfect location for exploring western Georgia's natural attractions.

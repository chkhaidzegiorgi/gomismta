About This Space

Welcome to Cotria, your peaceful mountain escape in Gomismta, Georgia. With stunning views, cozy interiors, and all the essentials, this cottage is perfect for a relaxing holiday. It includes 4 bedrooms, 1 sofa-bed (sleeps 9), a well-equipped kitchen, and a spacious living room with panoramic windows.
